How to request deletion for your BioBeing account

  1. Click the "Request deletion for BioBeing" button above, which points to this public deletion resource for BioBeing, developed by Charliee Tech and RailsShark Tech.
  2. If you prefer email, send a message from the email associated with your BioBeing account to railsshark@gmail.com, include your BioBeing username or registered email, and write "Delete my BioBeing account".
  3. We will reply to confirm your identity, and may ask a single verification question to protect your account from unauthorized deletion.
  4. After verification, we will proceed to remove your BioBeing account and the data you requested deleted. You will receive a confirmation when deletion is complete.

Delete Account URL used in your Google Play Data safety form: https://railsshark.github.io/RailsShark/delete

What data is deleted and what may be retained

Deleted on request
  • Account profile, including display name and profile photo
  • Credentials stored on our servers, where applicable (not plain passwords)
  • User generated content tied only to your account, such as private notes and uploads
  • Preferences and app settings linked to your BioBeing account
May be retained
  • System logs and security audit entries, retained for up to 90 days for fraud prevention
  • Transactional or billing records kept for legal or accounting reasons, up to 3 years
  • Backups that include your data, overwritten or scrubbed during routine retention cycles (typically within 30 to 90 days)
  • Content that cannot be removed without affecting other users, for example shared content

If you require precise retention dates for a specific item, include a note in your deletion request and we will provide exact timelines in our confirmation.

Processing time and verification

We will acknowledge deletion requests within 3 business days. We aim to complete deletions within 30 days of verification, unless legal obligations require a longer retention period. If we cannot fully delete certain data for legal reasons, we will explain what is retained and why.

Contact and additional references

For deletion requests by email: railsshark@gmail.com. Mention your BioBeing username or the email associated with your BioBeing account to speed up verification.
