RailsShark, Data Deletion and Account Removal

If you want your RailsShark account and associated data deleted, you can request deletion here. If you prefer email, contact us at the address below.

Request account deletion (self-serve) Email us: railsshark@gmail.com

How to request deletion

  1. Click the "Request account deletion" button above to open the self-serve deletion page. Follow the on-screen steps to confirm which account you want removed.
  2. If you prefer email, send a message from the email address registered with your RailsShark account to railsshark@gmail.com. Include your app username or registered email, and the words "Delete my account".
  3. We will confirm receipt and may ask a single verification question to protect your account from unauthorized requests.
  4. Once verified, we begin removal of your account and specified data. You will receive a confirmation email when the deletion is complete.

If you cannot access your registered email, include any other info that proves account ownership and we will guide you through verification steps.

What is deleted and what we keep

Data that will be deleted
  • Account profile (display name, profile photo)
  • Account credentials stored on our servers (we do not store plain text passwords)
  • User-generated content, such as posts, uploaded media, and profile fields, unless it is shared with other users and cannot be removed without affecting them
  • Personal identifiers used to link you to the account (email, phone number) from active records
  • App-specific settings and preferences tied to your account
Data that may be retained
  • System logs and audit records required for security and fraud prevention, retained for up to 90 days
  • Transactional records needed for legal or billing reasons, retained for up to 3 years where applicable
  • Backups that include your data, which are retained for a limited period; such backups are overwritten or scrubbed during routine retention cycles (typically within 30 to 90 days)
  • Data that cannot be deleted for legal reasons or that includes content contributed by other users

If you need exact retention timelines for a specific item, mention it in your request and we will include precise dates in our confirmation.

Processing time and verification

We aim to acknowledge deletion requests within 3 business days, and to complete deletions within 30 days of verification, unless a longer retention period is required by law or to resolve disputes. If we cannot comply fully, we will explain what is retained and why.
